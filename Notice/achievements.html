Event: Smart India Hackathon (SIH)

Organiser: Ministry of Education India

Description: The Smart India Hackathon (SIH) is a government-led initiative in India, inviting students from various technical backgrounds to solve real-world challenges. Participants form teams and work intensively over a few days to develop innovative solutions to problem statements provided by government departments and industries. With mentorship from experts, teams refine their ideas, create prototypes, and prepare presentations. At the end of the hackathon, solutions are evaluated by a panel of judges, with winning teams awarded prizes and recognition. SIH promotes collaboration between academia, industry, and government, fostering innovation and entrepreneurship among students while addressing pressing societal issues.

Event: Hack IT Sapiens

Organiser: Poornima College of Engineering

Description: Hack IT Sapiens at Poornima College of Engineering is an annual hackathon event aimed at fostering innovation and problem-solving among students in the field of information technology (IT). Hosted by Poornima College of Engineering, the event brings together students from various technical backgrounds to collaborate and develop creative solutions to real-world challenges in the IT domain. Participants form teams and work intensively over a specified period, typically spanning a few days, to brainstorm ideas, develop prototypes, and present their solutions to a panel of judges. The hackathon encourages interdisciplinary collaboration, providing participants with mentorship, resources, and networking opportunities. Through Hack IT Sapiens, Poornima College of Engineering aims to nurture talent, promote entrepreneurship, and showcase the innovative potential of students in the IT field.

Organiser: Six Sigma Yellow Belt

Description: he Six Sigma Yellow Belt (SSYB) professional certificate is a credential awarded to individuals who have completed training and demonstrated proficiency in the foundational concepts of Six Sigma methodology at the Yellow Belt level. Six Sigma is a data-driven approach to process improvement and quality management that aims to minimize defects and variations in processes, thereby enhancing efficiency and customer satisfaction. It employs a structured problem-solving methodology and statistical tools to identify, analyze, and eliminate sources of variation and waste within an organization.
